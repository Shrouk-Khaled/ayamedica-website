ABOUT AYAMEDICA

About us

At Ayamedica, we aspire to transform the healthcare industry by improving access and management of healthcare information. Ayamedica encompasses the most comprehensive medical history application with the sole purpose of easing the burden of managing health information on multiple parties.                                                                                                                        

Ayamedica provides the ultimate solution by connecting the patient with their own medical history, as provided by the medical professional, in a method that is secure and discrete. Now, patients can have access to their medical records through the Ayamedica’s mobile and web application, facilitated and designed with a simple user interface that promotes security, transparency, and protection of patient health data. Your health care just got simpler and easier to manage!
