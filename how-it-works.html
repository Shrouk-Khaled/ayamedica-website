Individuals can now manage their personal medical history records, and those of their family members, through the Ayamedica application available on their Mobiles.
‍
Ayamedica allows end-users to schedule consultations (online and in-person), manage their medical records, provide authorization requests, and change their PCP’s.

Ayamedica is present within the patient’s stay in hospitals and/or clinics. When the patient accepts the terms and regulations of the application, they may begin to facilitate communication. Hospitals and Clinics are able to use

Ayamedica as an application for advertising, in parallel to the facilitation of rapid organization; archiving medical records completed at a faster rate on a user-friendly platform, all of which are digital, and utterly secured through rapid authorization requests.

Ayamedica guarantees families an ease of mind when it comes to accessing the medical records and diagnosis results of their children within their schooling system. When the parent of the student accepts the terms and regulations of the application, they may begin to facilitate a seamless network between academic faculty, students, and parents to increase efficiency and decrease worry.
